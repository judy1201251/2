spring

Spring is the season of renewal, with rising temperatures, blooming flowers, and chirping birds. People sow seeds and start new plans, filled with hope and vitality. It marks the beginning of a vibrant year.

summer

Summer brings high temperatures and strong sunlight, with lush greenery and thriving crops. People enjoy swimming and staying cool while relishing sunny days and refreshing drinks. It’s the most energetic time of the year.

autumn

Autumn sees cooler weather and golden leaves, painting the land with a harvest scene. People focus on reaping and storing crops, enjoying the fruits of their labor. Its tranquil beauty reflects maturity and quietness.

winter

Winter is the coldest season, with dormant nature and occasional snow in some regions. People wear warm clothes and gather indoors for warmth. It’s a quiet season, yet festivals and reunions make it cozy and joyful.
